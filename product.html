Product and Services

  • Early diagnosis: Enables the distinction between diseases like Parkinson's and Parkinsonism at an early stage, aiding in timely intervention.
  • Improved monitoring: Non-invasive analysis allows for repeatable monitoring of disease progression and treatment efficacy.
  • Risk assessment: Vocal analysis helps identify the risk levels of conditions like dysphagia, enabling early intervention to prevent complications.
  • Substance intake monitoring: Rapid and non-invasive analysis facilitates monitoring of psychotropic substance intake for purposes like road safety.


Product Roadmap

In the five-year period, the product Voicewise ST for Parkinson’s disease will be finalized, then that for diabetes, pertussis, etc., developing in parallel the Voicewise EU version (end user), Caregifer with specially designed hardware.

The brief introduction

The Idea

AiSense in the ST version for the structures will provide indicators on the state of health, suggest diagnosis, offer services for monitoring pathologies and therapies.
Allows doctors to consult the anonymized database with evidence of patients with similar characteristics, diagnosis. Allows you to enter data from other analyzes or clinical evidence; allows you to consult the patient’s history and related deductions made by the expert system of AiSense also via Chat BOT (for the collection of further wellness data or disturbances reported by the patient).

Final users

AiSense

AiSense in the user version (EU) consists of an external unit equipped with a microphone and signal processing unit that connects to the internet and to the user’s smartphone via an APP, recording the voice samples in the few minutes of training required and reporting a synthetic indicator of the state of wellness.
The user can view the details and advice of the VoiceWise team and possibly book a consultation (using the appropriate network of associated specialists) via Chat BOT (for collecting additional wellness data or disturbances reported by the patient) and optionally a telephone. At the end it will be advised to consult your doctor to carry out the necessary investigations.

The service is provided in subscription mode.

Specific for the elderly

Caregifer

The Caregifer version, mainly dedicated to the elderly, of a multifunction wrist device is under study: a smartwatch connected to the service center, capable of detecting abnormal states (heartbeats, fall etc.), making periodic questions (for medical purposes), acquire images from the camera (expiry of products, medicines, etc.) and other support functions for use by family members (video call etc.) or the patient.
Come PLUS will have integrated the connection to the VoiceWise cloud system for periodic voice analysis (independently or assisted by the family member) to check the patient’s status or to be integrated into the disease prevention and progression system made available by VoiceWise for doctors GP.
