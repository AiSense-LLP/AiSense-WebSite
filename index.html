Our Services

Voice Analytics for Disease Screening

By analyzing the characteristics of speech, we can detect potential indications of certain diseases. Our advanced algorithms analyze voice patterns, intonation, and other acoustic features to identify possible health issues.

Pulse Characteristics for Disease Diagnosis

Through the analysis of pulse characteristics, we can gain insights into various health conditions. Our techniques enable us to detect irregularities in pulse patterns, providing valuable information for disease diagnosis.

Motor Movement Analysis

Motor movement analysis helps us assess motor function and detect anomalies that might indicate underlying health problems. By analyzing movement patterns and coordination, we can identify potential areas of concern.

Optical Face Image for Disease Screening

Our advanced image analysis algorithms analyze optical face images to screen for specific diseases. By examining facial features and subtle cues, we can identify potential health risks.

NEP Implementation Consultation

AiSense offers consultancy services to colleges and universities to support the effective implementation of the National Education Policy 2020. Our aim is to facilitate the transformation of educational systems, empowering students and educators alike.

Naturopathy Consultation

Our online consultancy services provide you with the convenience and flexibility to access naturopathic expertise from the comfort of your own home or any location of your choice through secure and confidential virtual consultations.
