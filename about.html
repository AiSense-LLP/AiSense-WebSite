AiSense LLP is an Indian company specializing in bio-signal analytics for early disease screening. We are dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions to improve healthcare outcomes. In addition to our expertise in bio-signal analytics, we also provide consultancy services in education and research, helping educational institutions implement the National Education Policy 2020 effectively. At AiSense, we believe in empowering college and university students to contribute to cost-effective innovations and provide job-oriented skill courses to equip them with the necessary knowledge.

  • Core Focus: Leveraging technology and innovative solutions to improve healthcare outcomes
  • Additional Services: Consultancy services in education and research
  • Education Focus: Implementing the National Education Policy 2020 effectively
  • Empowerment: Encouraging college and university students to contribute to cost-effective innovations
  • Skill Development: Providing job-oriented skill courses to equip students with necessary knowledge
